Barfi

Ingredients:

  • Khoya
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom pods
  • A handful of chopped nuts

Recipe:

  1. Cook crumbled khoya until golden and leaving pan edges.
  2. Gradually mix in warm sugar syrup, stirring constantly.
  3. Spread mixture in greased pan, garnish with nuts if desired.
  4. Cool, cut, and savor your homemade Barfi delight.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Khoya
  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Ghee or Oil
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom pods
  • Rose water or Kewra water
  • Lemon juice

Recipe:

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Simmer 2 cups of sugar and 1.5 cups of water with cardamom for 10 minutes; add lemon juice and rose water, then keep warm.
  2. Make Dough: Mix 1 cup grated khoya, 2 tbsp flour, and 1/4 tsp baking powder; form a soft dough with milk and roll into smooth balls.
  3. Fry Balls: Fry the dough balls in moderately hot ghee or oil until golden brown, then drain.
  4. Soak and Serve: Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours, garnish, and serve.
